ARTICLES OF ASSOCIATION

1. ESTABLISHMENT & PARTIES +
These Articles of Association have been drawn up to determine the principles of establishment, management, and operation of the company operating under the name ALPHATECH Holding. The company was officially founded on July 13, 2025.
2. COMPANY TITLE & PURPOSE +
The official title of the company is ALPHATECH Holding. The primary objectives are: developing software frameworks, producing electronic hardware systems, executing advanced technological R&D, implementing innovative training networks, and deploying high-tier industrial solutions.
3. FIELDS OF ACTIVITY +
The Holding operates dynamically across these sectors: Software technologies, hardware design, advanced R&D, corporate educational structures, UI/UX product design, commercial operations, global logistics, info-communication systems, and critical cyber defense platforms.
4. COMPANY MANAGEMENT +
4.1 Board of Directors: The supreme decision-making body of the company. Strategic investments, funding allocations, and macro plans are approved here.
4.2 Chairman: Holds the executive power to make urgent and critical strategic decisions when necessary, subject to subsequent Board approval.
5. EXECUTIVE MANAGEMENT +
5.1 CVO (Chief Vision Officer): Sets the long-term vision and macro direction.
5.2 CGO (Chief Growth Officer): Responsible for scaling commercial assets, pipelines, and market sales.
5.3 CTO (Chief Technology Officer): Commands software, hardware architecture, and overall technical development.
6. CAPITAL & FINANCIAL PROVISIONS +
6.1 Company capital and financial resources are fully managed under Board directives.
6.2 Board approval is strictly mandatory for all large-scale expenditures.
6.3 Every engineering project is governed by its own isolated budget and expenditure ceiling.
7. ORGANIZATIONAL STRUCTURE +
The organization operates using a hybrid system based on functional departments and cross-functional project teams. Departments work autonomously but collaborate closely during cross-platform product cycles. Core sectors: R&D, Education, Hardware, Software, Product Design, Commercial Support, Sales, Logistics, Information, and Defense systems.
8. PROJECT LIFECYCLE +
Projects systematically proceed through these gates: Idea Conceptualization → Formal Approval → Engineering/Technical Design → Production & Dev → Threat & Quality Testing → Marketing & Launch. No asset or resource can be allocated without explicit project validation.
9. CONFIDENTIALITY & SECURITY +
All internal architectures, databases, and intellectual properties are strictly confidential. Continuous data protection and systemic defense perimeters are heavily maintained by the Defense and Information-Communication departments.
10. FINAL CLAUSE +
This foundational charter serves as the primary governing constitution of ALPHATECH Holding and is legally binding across all corporate actions. It can only be amended by an official resolution of the Board of Directors.
